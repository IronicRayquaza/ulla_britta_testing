# 4K Screenshot Studio

4K Screenshot Studio is a Chrome extension designed to take crisp, high-definition captures of any webpage and provide an immediate, built-in environment for turning those captures into clean, ready-to-share product graphics.

## Key Features

- **True 4K Resolution Capture**
  Unlike tools that compress or shrink images to fit smaller window dimensions, this extension captures and handles your page at its native, scrolling high-definition resolution. This ensures your final exported file maintains crisp fidelity—perfect for important presentations or official product launches.

- **Interactive Studio Editor**
  Once a screenshot is captured, it instantly opens in a Figma-style, infinite-panning canvas right inside your browser. You have full control to annotate, apply macOS or Windows frame overlays, adjust shadows, and select from a diverse collection of premium solid, mesh, and geometric background templates.

- **Native Platform Resizing**
  Preparing an image for X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Dribbble, or Instagram? Selecting a platform target natively expands the background canvas to perfectly match the required aspect ratio. It wraps around your uncompressed screenshot, ensuring you never have to manually crop or lose resolution just to fit a social media upload requirement.

## Why use 4K Screenshot Studio?

There are many screenshot tools available, but 4K Screenshot Studio was built with a practical focus on **quality preservation and workflow efficiency.** 

* **No Server Processing:** Every capture, manipulation, and export happens 100% locally on your machine. There are no external cloud uploads, watermarks, or data trackers. 
* **Prevents Compression:** It doesn't aggressively compress your screenshots for the sake of rendering speed. Your 4K captures remain 4K throughout the entire editing pipeline.
* **Streamlined Showcase Generation:** Normally, creating a product showcase requires capturing a screen, importing it into a tool like Figma or Photoshop, building a background, and setting up precise aspect ratio drop shadows. This extension reduces that 10-minute workflow into just a few clicks without ever leaving your browser.

## Installation Instructions

1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
3. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder containing this extension's files.
4. Pin the extension to your toolbar, capture a page, and start designing.
